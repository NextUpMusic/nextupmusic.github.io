The debate over Fortnite’s use of the popular dances accelerated in the days after the “Milly Rock” appeared to be included on Fortnite Battle Royale’s Season 5 Battle Pass in July. The game, which boasts more than 200 million players, $1 billion in revenue and a reputation for hogging boyfriends' attention, is free for download. But Fortnite makes its money through in-game purchases and “Battle Passes,” which is how users could unlock the “Swipe It” dance.
